Yuhong Deng

I am a master student in Tsinghua university since 2020, advised by Prof. Xueqian Wang.
Previously, I received bachelor degree from Tsinghua University in 2020. I was a research assistant at the department of computer science, Tsinghua University from 2017 to 2020, working with Prof. Huaping Liu. I have interned in Tencent and Shanghai AI Lab etc.

My research focuses on robot vision, reinforcement learning and deep learning, to equip robots with intelligent learning, manipulation and perception capabality. Feel free to reach out for collaboration and discussion of research ideas.
